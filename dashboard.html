Paradox Profile (Radar Chart)

The Paradox Profile shows your leadership pattern across five tensions that every modern leader must navigate. Each axis reflects your relative pull toward one pole or the other, revealing where you show strong preferences, where you balance competing priorities, and how consistently you apply your leadership style across different contexts.

Shows whether you prioritize mission‑aligned, outcome‑driven digital investments (Strategic Intent) or rapid adoption of emerging technologies to maintain momentum (Technology Enthusiasm).

Shows whether you emphasize organization‑wide standardization and integration (Enterprise Scale) or empower teams to experiment and innovate locally (Local Innovation).

Shows whether you lead transformation through people, skills, and culture (People & Culture) or through strong technical foundations and architecture (Platforms & Architecture).

Shows whether you focus on automating existing processes for efficiency (Automation Efficiency) or redesigning services end‑to‑end for better outcomes and user experience (Service Redesign).

Shows whether you push for rapid progress and experimentation (Innovation Speed) or emphasize governance, safety, trust, and responsible AI (Risk, Security & Trust).
