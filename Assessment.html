Strategic Intent

SI4. I challenge teams to demonstrate strategic alignment before approving digital work.

Technology Enthusiasm

TE1. I actively explore emerging technologies to identify new opportunities.
TE2. I encourage experimentation with new tools even before full strategic clarity.
TE3. I stay informed about rapid technology shifts and trends.
TE4. I support pilots that test new technologies for potential value.

Enterprise Scale

ES1. When launching digital transformation, I design initiatives to scale across the entire organization.
ES2. I challenge teams on how solutions will integrate with core platforms and processes.

Local Innovation

LI1. I encourage experimentation even if solutions are not yet enterprise-ready.

Platforms & Architecture

PA1. I emphasize cloud strategy, architecture, and long-term sustainability.
PA2. I ensure alignment with data platforms, integration standards, and APIs.
PA3. I oversee cost control, FinOps, and architectural guardrails.
PA4. I strengthen platforms and core systems to improve resilience.

Automation Efficiency

AE1. I prioritize automation of manual, high-volume, error-prone processes.
AE2. I focus on throughput, cost reduction, and accuracy.
AE3. I emphasize productivity gains and operational efficiency.
AE4. I favour repeatable automation patterns across functions.

Service Redesign

SR1. I redesign end-to-end journeys for customers or staff.
SR2. I challenge teams to rethink workflows, not digitize old ones.
SR3. I prioritize experience, accessibility, and service quality.
SR4. I assess automation impacts on trust, equity, and long-term outcomes.

Innovation Speed

IS2. I encourage AI experimentation to maintain competitiveness.
IS3. I streamline approvals when timelines are under pressure.
IS4. I prefer progress with managed risk over delays.

Risk, Security & Trust

RT1. I embed privacy-by-design, security-by-design, and responsible AI.
RT2. I maintain strong identity, access, and zero-trust strategies.
